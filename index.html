About Us

OM Architects is renowned interior design firm that has executed prestigious projects in Ahmedabad and Surat.

Founded by Reena Partiwala / Himali Shah, Om, has been shaping the dream homes for esteemed clients from various walks of life including residential, commercial, office spaces and restaurants. The design style focuses on creating the luxury spaces while providing utmost functionality suiting client’s aspirations.

The designs are exclusive, contemporary, still possess a timeless style and unique vibrant appeal. Every space created is added with an aura of OM’s design statement

Our passion to constantly pursue fresh inspiration for innovative designs, meticulous project planning & execution and premium quality materials utilization has earned us recognition from our esteemed clientele.

We take pride in our work ethic, our eye for innovative designs and our commitment towards shaping clients dreams spaces.
